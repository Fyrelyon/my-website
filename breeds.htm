Foxopedia Wiki: Breeds of Foxes

There are 37 discovered species of foxes, 12 of which belonging to the "true fox" genus. Some examples include the red fox, the most common of foxes sporting a red coat, the gray fox, another common breed which has a grayish coat, the arctic fox, which has white fur to blend in with winter months, and the fennec fox, which is small and lives in deserts.
